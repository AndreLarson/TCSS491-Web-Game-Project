    CONTROLS:
  • (WASD) to move or crouch
  • (Space) to jump. Double tap to double jump! You are able to jump next to walls!
  • (Shift) to roll/dodge
  • (Left Click/P) to melee attack with your sword
  • (Right Click) Position your mouse and right click to shoot an arrow if you have any

    • ADVANCED COMBAT:
  • Clicking more than once will do a 2 attack combo chain!
  • You can move while crouching (S) and also attack while crouching.
  • You can pick up arrows that hit the environment by walking up to it.
